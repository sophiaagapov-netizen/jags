About JAGS International

JAGS International is an initiative dedicated to advancing glaucoma care through outreach, education, collaboration, and innovation.

Founded by Dr. Julia Agapov, JAGS International supports programs that connect physicians, strengthen local capacity, and improve access to quality glaucoma care worldwide.

“ Through collaboration and education, we can create a world where fewer people lose their sight to glaucoma. ” — Dr. Julia Agapov

OUR PILLARS

GLOBAL OUTREACH

International collaboration, surgical training, physician education, and humanitarian service.

GLAUCOMA SCHOOL

Developing a future platform for glaucoma education, certification, and hands-on training through global partnerships.

Glaucoma School

Training the next generation of glaucoma care leaders

The inaugural JAGS Glaucoma School program in Uzbekistan brings together international faculty, practical training, and regional collaboration to strengthen glaucoma education across the Alliance.

Educational Sponsor

Supporting the inaugural JAGS Glaucoma School program in Uzbekistan.

  • 3-5 Day Intensive Course
  • International Faculty
  • Lectures & Wet Labs
  • Surgical Training
  • Official Completion Certificate
  • CME Credits (where approved)

Rotating Host Model
